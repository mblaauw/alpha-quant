difficulty: advanced

The Technical Score is a weighted composite of five sub-scores that together grade a stock's technical setup. Each sub-score ranges from 0.0 to 1.0 and the final composite is clamped to [0.0, 1.0].

The five components and their weights are: trend (31.25%), RSI (25%), MACD (18.75%), volume (12.5%), and ATR (12.5%). Trend measures distance from the 50-day EMA — above +5% scores 1.0, above the EMA scores 0.6, within −5% scores 0.3, below scores 0.0. Momentum is evaluated as a separate signal (63-day return) and combined with the technical score in the M8 ranking system.

The composite is then used in the M8 ranking system, where it is combined with insider and momentum signals to produce a final ranking score. A Technical Score above 0.5 is required to pass the initial gate.

**Key takeaway:** Six technical factors are weighted into a single 0–1 score. Trend and momentum carry the most weight.
