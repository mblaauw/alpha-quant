Momentum trading is based on the observation that securities that have performed well tend to continue performing well in the near term. It is one of the most robust and well-documented market anomalies.

In Alpha Quant, momentum is measured as the 63-trading-day (≈3 month) return. A gain above 20% scores a perfect 1.0. Gains between 10% and 20% score 0.8, and between 5% and 10% score 0.6. Small positive returns score 0.5, small losses score 0.3, and large losses (below −15%) score 0.0.

Momentum contributes to both the Technical Score (20% weight) and the composite ranking score. It is one of two universally available signals (along with technical) that are always present in the ranking system.

**Key takeaway:** Momentum measures 3-month return. Strong momentum increases the likelihood of continued outperformance.
